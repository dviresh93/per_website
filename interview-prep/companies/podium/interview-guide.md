# Podium - Senior AI Engineer

## Job Details

**Company:** Podium
**Role:** Senior AI Engineer
**Location:** Lehi, Utah (Remote OK)
**Interview Type:** 15-minute phone screen with senior engineering leader

---

## What They Do

Podium builds a platform for local businesses with AI employees that convert leads. Their AI agents handle millions of conversations per month across multiple channels.

---

## What They Need

### Core Responsibilities
- Build and scale AI Agent platform (millions of conversations/month)
- Design APIs, services, infrastructure for multi-turn, cross-channel interactions
- Own full lifecycle: architecture → deployment → reliability
- Rapid prototyping with live data, continuous iteration
- Implement observability, monitoring for agent accuracy and latency
- Collaborate across teams (engineers, PMs, AI/ML scientists)

### Required Skills
- **6-10+ years software engineering** (Go, Python, or Elixir)
- **1+ years deploying/maintaining AI agents in production** (tools, APIs, workflows)
- Distributed/high-throughput systems experience
- Strong problem-solving, comfortable with ambiguity
- Cross-functional communication skills
- Full-stack mindset (infrastructure → APIs → UI)

### Nice-to-Have
- LLM evaluations (accuracy, safety, reliability, cost)
- Prompt design, context engineering, guardrails
- Agent frameworks (LangGraph, CrewAI, AutoGen)
- Multi-step reasoning, memory, orchestration
- Fine-tuning and data curation

---

## Phone Screen Format (15 Minutes)

### Structure
1. **2 min:** Intro + role overview from interviewer
2. **5 min:** Background questions
   - Walk through current role
   - Why this role/company
   - High-level technical experience
3. **5 min:** Technical depth questions
   - AI agent architecture
   - Production challenges
   - Scale/reliability experience
   - Framework familiarity
4. **3 min:** Your questions + close

### What They're Assessing
- ✅ Do you have production AI agent experience?
- ✅ Can you build scalable, reliable systems?
- ✅ Do you understand multi-turn conversational AI?
- ✅ Are you comfortable with ambiguity and rapid iteration?
- ✅ Can you communicate clearly across teams?

---

## Key Questions to Expect

### Background
1. "Walk me through your current role at Grid CoOperator. What does the AI system do?"
2. "Why are you interested in Podium? What excites you about conversational AI?"

### Technical Depth
3. "Describe the architecture of your AI agent system. How does it handle queries?"
4. "What was the hardest production issue you faced? How did you debug it?"
5. "Our platform handles millions of conversations per month. How do you ensure reliability and accuracy at scale?"
6. "Have you worked with agent frameworks like LangGraph or LangChain? How do you approach multi-turn conversations?"
7. "Tell me about your experience with distributed systems or high-throughput backends."

### Collaboration
8. "How do you work with non-technical stakeholders? Give an example."
9. "Describe a time you had to make a technical decision with incomplete information."
