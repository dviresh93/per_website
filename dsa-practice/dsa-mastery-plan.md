# DSA Mastery Plan - Pattern-Based Learning

## Philosophy
Master 10-15 problems at a time through repetition before moving to the next batch. Focus on understanding patterns deeply rather than solving many problems once.

## Learning Approach

### Phase Structure
1. **Initial Solve** (Days 1-3): Solve each problem, understand the solution
2. **Pattern Recognition** (Days 4-7): Solve again from scratch, identify patterns
3. **Speed Building** (Days 8-12): Solve again, focus on implementation speed
4. **Variation Practice** (Days 13-15): Modify constraints, think of edge cases
5. **Teaching Phase** (Days 16-18): Explain solutions out loud, write explanations
6. **Final Sprint** (Days 19-21): Solve all 10-15 problems in one session

### Daily Routine
- **Morning**: Solve 2-3 problems without looking at previous solutions
- **Evening**: Review your solution vs optimal, note patterns in journal
- **Weekend**: Full batch review - solve all problems timed

## Tips for Success

1. **Don't Look at Solutions Too Early**: Struggle for at least 30 minutes
2. **Understand, Don't Memorize**: Focus on WHY the solution works
3. **Code Without IDE**: Practice on paper/whiteboard occasionally
4. **Verbalize Your Thought Process**: Talk through solutions out loud
5. **Time Yourself**: Build speed gradually, don't rush initially
6. **Review Mistakes**: Keep a mistake journal
7. **Take Breaks**: If stuck for 45+ mins, take a break and return
8. **Consistency Over Volume**: 2 problems deeply > 10 problems shallowly
