## Job Description

### Company Mission
Reinvent the way people learn, starting with language. Building human-level, AI-powered tutor in your pocket - conversation-first experience for language learning. Number one language learning app in South Korea, serving learners across many markets and 15+ languages.

### About the Role
Pivotal role developing future of language learning and creating most effective path to language fluency. Develop innovative product experiences powered by LLMs and deploy them to millions of users.

### Responsibilities
- Develop and deploy LLM-powered language learning products across full stack
- Enhance quality and performance of existing AI-powered features within Speak
- Collaborate cross-functionally with Engineering, Applied ML, Product, Design, Content
- Refine process for building LLM apps: prompting, experimentation/evaluation, LLM Ops, quality/performance measurement
- Scale existing product features to many more users and languages
- Full stack and beyond: evaluate models, test prompts, develop/iterate on:
  - Conversational onboarding
  - Lesson experiences
  - Grammatical assessment and feedback
  - Personalized lessons

### Requirements
- 3-5+ years experience in full stack/backend, product-focused software engineering
- Proficiency in React/Node/TypeScript AND Python
- Real-world experience developing and deploying LLM apps
- Strong understanding of what works and what doesn't with LLMs
- Keen intuition for improving performance and output quality of LLM systems
- Experience with LLM Ops and tools: vector databases, RAG, prompt ops
- Strong product intuition: think broadly and cross-functionally about innovative LLM-powered capabilities
- Ability to work independently and build at high velocity

### Why Speak
- Join at magical time: fast growth, Series C, product-market fit achieved
- Do life's work with people you'll love: tight-knit team, strong craft focus
- Global nature: Live in 40+ countries, offices in SF, Ljubljana, Seoul, Tokyo
- Impact millions: Help people achieve life-changing language learning goals
